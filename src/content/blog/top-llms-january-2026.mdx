### New This Month

**GPT-5.2** (Dec 10) — OpenAI's major winter release. 400K context window, significantly better reasoning than 4o, and a more aggressive price point. Early adoption is strong but hasn't yet dethroned Claude for coding workflows.

**Devstral 2** (Dec 9) — Mistral's developer-focused model. 262K context, competitive pricing, and strong code generation. A serious entry in the mid-tier coding model space.

**Gemini 3 Flash Preview** (Dec 17) — Google's next-gen speed model drops in late December. Early access users report absurd throughput numbers. Expect this one to climb fast in February.

### The Story

Claude 3.5 Sonnet starts 2026 exactly where it ended 2025: on top. Developers have built entire toolchains around it, and switching costs are real. But the challengers are circling. DeepSeek V3 offers 90% of the quality at 10% of the price. GPT-5.2 brings OpenAI back into the conversation with a genuinely improved model. And Google's Flash lineup is eating the high-volume, low-cost segment alive.
