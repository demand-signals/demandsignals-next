One of the most common mistakes businesses make when approaching Generative Engine Optimization is treating all AI platforms the same. They optimize for "AI search" as a monolithic category and assume the same content and signals will work everywhere.

That assumption is wrong. ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews each draw from different data sources, weight different signals, and favor different content structures. A strategy that gets you cited on Perplexity may not move the needle on Google AI Overviews, and vice versa.

Here is what each platform actually looks for, and how to optimize for all three simultaneously.

## How ChatGPT Selects Citations

ChatGPT with web browsing — now the default mode for ChatGPT Plus and Enterprise subscribers — searches the web in real-time when answering queries. Understanding how it selects which sources to cite is the first step to getting your business mentioned.

### What ChatGPT Prioritizes

**Domain authority and trust signals.** ChatGPT tends to cite sources from domains it perceives as authoritative. Established business websites with substantial content, consistent NAP information, and a history of being cited by other sources get preference over thin sites.

**Review aggregation data.** For local business queries ("best plumber in Sacramento," "top-rated HVAC near me"), ChatGPT heavily weights review data from Google, Yelp, and industry-specific platforms. Businesses with high review volume and positive sentiment are dramatically more likely to be cited.

**Structured, factual content.** ChatGPT's citation tendency favors pages that present information in clear, factual, scannable formats. FAQ pages, service descriptions with specific details, and how-to guides with step-by-step structures get cited more frequently than narrative marketing copy.

**Third-party mentions.** Being mentioned on other reputable sites — local business directories, industry associations, news outlets, chamber of commerce listings — increases the probability that ChatGPT includes your business in synthesized answers.

### Optimization Tactics for ChatGPT

- Maintain active review profiles on Google, Yelp, and industry platforms with consistent response to reviews
- Build FAQ pages with specific, detailed answers to common customer questions
- Ensure NAP consistency across all online presences
- Get listed on authoritative local and industry directories
- Publish content that answers specific questions with clear, factual responses

## How Perplexity Selects Sources

Perplexity operates differently from ChatGPT. It runs its own search index and ranks sources based on a combination of traditional search signals and content quality indicators.

### What Perplexity Prioritizes

**Content recency.** Perplexity shows a strong preference for recently published or updated content. A blog post from last month frequently outranks an older page with the same information. This makes publishing cadence a competitive factor.

**Content depth and specificity.** Perplexity favors long-form, detailed content over surface-level summaries. Pages that go deep on a topic — with specific data points, examples, methodology details, and original analysis — get cited over generic overviews.

**Source diversity.** Perplexity tries to synthesize answers from multiple sources. It is more likely to cite your business if your content provides a unique angle or data point that other sources do not cover. Saying the same thing everyone else says is not enough.

**Technical structure.** Clean HTML, proper heading hierarchy, schema markup, and fast load times correlate with higher citation rates on Perplexity. The platform's crawler appears to favor technically well-implemented pages.

### Optimization Tactics for Perplexity

- Publish fresh content regularly — weekly blog posts, monthly data updates, quarterly industry analysis
- Go deep rather than broad — 1,500-word detailed guides outperform 500-word summaries
- Include original data, analysis, or perspective that is not available elsewhere
- Implement comprehensive schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Service, HowTo)
- Maintain fast page load times and clean technical implementation

## How Google AI Overviews Work

Google AI Overviews are the AI-generated summaries that appear above traditional search results for an increasing percentage of queries. Because they are Google's own product, they leverage Google's existing search infrastructure.

### What AI Overviews Prioritize

**Traditional SEO signals.** AI Overviews draw heavily from pages that already rank well in traditional search. If you are on page one for a query, you are a candidate for the AI Overview. If you are on page three, you almost certainly are not. This makes AI Overviews the most SEO-dependent of the three platforms.

**Google Business Profile data.** For local queries, AI Overviews incorporate GBP data — reviews, business hours, photos, services, Q&A. A complete, actively managed GBP is essential for local AI Overview citations.

**Schema markup.** Google has the most sophisticated schema parsing of any search platform. FAQPage schema, LocalBusiness schema, Service schema, and Review schema all increase the likelihood that your content is selected for an AI Overview.

**E-E-A-T signals.** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness — Google's quality rater guidelines — apply to AI Overviews as strongly as they do to traditional search. Author bylines, about pages, credentials, and external citations all contribute.

### Optimization Tactics for AI Overviews

- Build your traditional SEO foundation first — rankings feed AI Overview selection
- Optimize and actively manage your Google Business Profile
- Implement comprehensive schema markup on every page
- Build E-E-A-T signals through author pages, credentials, and external mentions
- Create content that directly answers questions in concise, structured formats

## The Unified GEO Strategy

The good news: while each platform has different emphases, there is significant overlap. A unified strategy that covers all three platforms looks like this:

1. **Build your SEO foundation.** This is table stakes for AI Overviews and provides baseline authority signals for ChatGPT and Perplexity.

2. **Implement comprehensive schema markup.** All three platforms benefit from structured data, though Google uses it most directly.

3. **Publish deep, regularly updated content.** This is the primary signal for Perplexity and a supporting signal for the other two.

4. **Manage reviews actively.** Critical for ChatGPT citations and Google AI Overviews. A strong review profile with consistent response demonstrates active business management.

5. **Build citation diversity.** Directory listings, association memberships, and local press mentions strengthen authority signals across all platforms.

6. **Create platform-specific content formats.** FAQ pages for ChatGPT, long-form guides for Perplexity, schema-rich service pages for AI Overviews.
