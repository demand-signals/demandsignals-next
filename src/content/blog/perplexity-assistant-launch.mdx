Perplexity has been the quiet disruptor in search. While the industry debated whether ChatGPT or Google would win the AI search war, Perplexity built a dedicated answer engine that grew its user base tenfold in 2024. On January 27, 2025, they launched Perplexity Assistant — and it changes the game for local business discovery.

The core shift: Perplexity Assistant does not just find answers. It takes action. It can make restaurant reservations, fill out contact forms, initiate purchases, and complete multi-step tasks on the user's behalf. Search is no longer about giving users information and letting them act on it. Search is becoming the action itself.

## What Perplexity Assistant Actually Does

Perplexity Assistant combines the answer engine's existing capabilities — synthesized answers with source citations — with the ability to interact with websites and services on the user's behalf.

In practical terms:

**"Find me an Italian restaurant in Midtown with availability tonight for four"** — the Assistant searches for options, checks availability through online reservation systems, presents choices, and can book the table directly.

**"I need an emergency plumber in Folsom who can come today"** — the Assistant identifies plumbers with availability, checks reviews, and can initiate contact through the business's website or phone.

**"Compare prices for gutter cleaning from top-rated services near me"** — the Assistant finds services, extracts pricing from their websites, compiles a comparison, and can fill out quote request forms.

The key distinction from previous AI search: the user does not leave Perplexity to complete the task. The Assistant handles the entire workflow, from discovery to action.

## Why This Matters for Local Businesses

For the past twenty years, local business discovery followed a predictable pattern: customer searches, customer sees results, customer visits websites, customer makes contact. Each step in that funnel gave the business opportunities to influence the decision — through their website design, their content, their calls to action.

Perplexity Assistant compresses that funnel. The customer may never visit your website. The Assistant reads your website, evaluates your reviews, checks your availability, and presents a summary — along with the ability to take action — without the customer ever seeing your homepage.

This has three immediate implications:

### Your Website Becomes a Data Source, Not a Destination

When an AI assistant is reading your website on behalf of a user, the information architecture matters more than the visual design. Clear, structured information about your services, pricing, availability, and contact methods is what the Assistant extracts. Beautiful animations and hero images are invisible to it.

This does not mean visual design stops mattering — human visitors still come directly to your site. But it means your website needs to serve two audiences simultaneously: humans who browse and AI agents that read.

### Reviews Become Even More Critical

When a human user reads reviews on Google or Yelp, they can contextualize individual reviews, dismiss outliers, and form a nuanced opinion. When an AI assistant summarizes your reviews for a user, it distills them into a few sentences: "Rated 4.7 stars with 340 reviews. Customers praise fast response times and clear communication. Some mention higher prices compared to competitors."

The quality, volume, and specificity of your reviews directly determines how the AI characterizes your business. Businesses with detailed, recent reviews that mention specific services and outcomes get better AI summaries than businesses with sparse, generic reviews.

### Structured Data Is the API for AI Agents

Perplexity Assistant reads structured data — schema markup — as its primary source of machine-readable business information. LocalBusiness schema tells it who you are and where you operate. Service schema tells it what you do. FAQPage schema tells it how to answer questions about your business. Availability data tells it whether you can serve the customer right now.

Without structured data, the Assistant has to infer all of this from unstructured web content. With structured data, you are essentially providing an API that AI agents can consume directly.

## The Broader Trend: Agentic Search

Perplexity Assistant is not an isolated product. It is part of a broader trend toward agentic search — AI systems that do not just retrieve information but take actions on behalf of users.

Google is building the same capabilities into Gemini and Google Assistant. OpenAI is building them into ChatGPT. Apple is building them into Siri. Within the next 12-18 months, every major search and assistant platform will offer some form of agentic capability.

The businesses that are prepared — with structured data, online booking systems, clear service information, and strong review profiles — will be the ones these agents recommend and interact with. The businesses that are not prepared will be invisible to the fastest-growing segment of customer discovery.

## What to Do Right Now

### Audit Your Machine Readability

Run your website through Google's Rich Results Test and Schema Markup Validator. Check whether your business information is expressed in structured data that AI agents can parse. If it is not, implementing LocalBusiness, Service, and FAQPage schema should be your immediate priority.

### Build an Online Booking or Contact System

If an AI agent cannot complete the transaction on your behalf — booking an appointment, requesting a quote, scheduling a consultation — you lose the user to a competitor whose system allows it. Online booking is no longer a convenience feature; it is a discovery requirement.

### Ensure Review Completeness

Your review profile across Google, Yelp, and industry-specific platforms should include recent reviews that mention specific services, locations, and outcomes. AI agents synthesize these reviews into the summary that determines whether a user chooses your business. Our [AI review auto-responder system](/content-social/ai-review-auto-responders) helps maintain the response side of this equation.

### Create Direct-Answer Content

For every service you offer, your website should directly and clearly answer: What is it? What does it cost? How long does it take? Where do you offer it? What makes your approach different? These are the questions AI agents ask on behalf of users, and they need clear, extractable answers.

## What This Means for Your Business

Perplexity Assistant is an early signal of a future where AI agents mediate a significant share of customer discovery and booking. The businesses that prepare for agent-mediated discovery now will have a structural advantage as this capability expands across every major platform.

The investment required is not large — it is primarily about information architecture, structured data, and review management. But it needs to happen before the competitive landscape shifts, because the businesses that are AI-agent-ready first will be the default recommendations in a market where AI agents increasingly make the first cut.
