## 🚨 The Big News
Anthropic Launches "Claude for Small Business" on May 13th

Anthropic rolled out a new offering tailored specifically for small businesses, marking their biggest push yet into the SMB market. This comes as the AI race heats up with companies fighting for market share beyond enterprise customers.

**What's new:**
- New pricing tier designed for teams of 5-50 people
- Simplified onboarding that takes less than 10 minutes to set up
- Pre-built templates for common small business tasks like customer service, content creation, and data analysis
- Priority support and dedicated small business success team

**Why this matters:** Small businesses have been largely underserved in the AI space, with most providers focusing on enterprise deals. This could open Claude to millions of potential new users who've been priced out of premium AI tools.
