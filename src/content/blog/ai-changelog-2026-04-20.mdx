## Codex
Major AI coding platform gets enhanced integrations

🧠  
**New · Marketplace**  
**Install plugins straight from GitHub and local sources**  


Codex added marketplace support for installing plugin marketplaces from GitHub, git URLs, local directories, and direct marketplace.json URLs

. Now you can grab workflows and tools from wherever they live instead of hunting through official directories.

⚡  
**Improved · TUI**  
**Better command history with reverse search**  


Added TUI prompt history improvements, including Ctrl+R reverse search and local recall for accepted slash commands

. Finding that perfect command from three sessions ago just got a whole lot easier.

🛠️  
**New · Memory**  
**Full control over AI memory and cleanup**  


Added TUI and app-server controls for memory mode, memory reset/deletion, and memory-extension cleanup

. When your AI starts remembering too much or the wrong things, you can finally do something about it.

🔄  
**Improved · MCP**  
**Enhanced plugin architecture with parallel processing**  


Expanded MCP/plugin support with MCP Apps tool calls, namespaced MCP registration, parallel-call opt-in, and sandbox-state metadata for MCP servers

. Your plugins can now work together instead of waiting in line.

## ChatGPT Enterprise
Smarter fallback model and better Outlook integration

🤖  
**New · Models**  
**GPT-5.3 Instant Mini replaces the old fallback**  


GPT-5.3 Instant Mini now replaces GPT-5 Instant Mini as the fallback model users reach after hitting their rate limits for GPT-5.3 Instant, with more natural conversation, stronger writing and contextual awareness throughout chats

. When you hit your limits, the backup AI is actually worth using now.

📋  
**New · Enterprise**  
**Shared Outlook mailboxes and calendar support**  


The Outlook Email and Calendar apps for ChatGPT now support delegated team workflows for shared mailboxes and shared calendars, including reading messages, moving mail, sending plain-text email, and managing calendar events

. Your AI can finally handle the team inbox without breaking a sweat.
