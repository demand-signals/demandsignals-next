## Anthropic Partners With Gates Foundation

**Anthropic expands its AI access with a major $200 million partnership**

Anthropic announced a significant new partnership with the Gates Foundation on Thursday, May 14. The collaboration aims to democratize access to Claude AI models for humanitarian and development work across global health, education, and poverty alleviation initiatives.

**What's new:**
- $200 million commitment from the Gates Foundation over five years
- Dedicated Claude compute resources for foundation partners
- Custom training capabilities for domain-specific applications
- Priority access to new model releases for qualifying organizations

**Why this matters:** This partnership signals Anthropic's commitment to expanding Claude's reach beyond commercial applications into social impact work, potentially accelerating AI adoption in underserved communities and humanitarian contexts.
