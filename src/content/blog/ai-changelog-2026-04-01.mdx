## TL;DR

Google launched their new Gemini 3 model yesterday — think of it as their answer to ChatGPT but with some extra tricks like real-time music generation and video creation. If you're happy with your current AI setup, nothing urgent to change today.

## Google / Gemini

**What changed:**
• Released Gemini 3, their newest and smartest AI model that can handle text, images, and code
• Added "Nano Banana" for creating images from text descriptions 
• Launched Veo for making videos from text prompts
• Introduced Lyria 3 for generating music, plus Lyria RealTime for creating music on the fly
• Released new text-to-speech capabilities that sound more natural
• Added robotics features for controlling physical devices

**Why you should care:** Gemini 3 competes directly with ChatGPT and Claude for business tasks like writing and analysis. The image and video creation tools could replace some design work. The music generation is interesting but probably not essential for most businesses. The robotics stuff is mainly for developers building hardware products.

## The Bottom Line

Unless you're already deep into Google's ecosystem, there's no rush to switch from whatever AI you're currently using. Gemini 3 looks solid but ChatGPT and Claude aren't suddenly obsolete. If you're curious about AI-generated images or videos for marketing, Google's new tools might be worth testing out.
